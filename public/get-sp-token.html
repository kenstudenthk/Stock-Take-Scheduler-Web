Get Your SharePoint Cookie

SharePoint uses session cookies (not Bearer tokens) for authentication.

Step 1 — Open the SharePoint list in Network tab

  1. Open Stock Take Record 2025 ↗
  2. Press F12 → click Network tab
  3. In the filter box, type RenderListData
  4. Press F5 to reload the page
  5. Click any POST request that appears
  6. Click Headers → scroll to Request Headers
  7. Find the cookie row — click the value to select all, then Ctrl+C to copy
